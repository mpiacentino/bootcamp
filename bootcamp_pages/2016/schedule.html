Schedule

Below is the class schedule. A few notes:

  • All regular meetings except Friday are in the Gates-Thomas 135. This includes the evening exercises, when students work together under the guidance of the course staff. On Friday, we will meet in 115 Gates-Thomas.
  • To allow attendance at International Student Orientation, incoming international grad students will skip sessions colored in purple and will have make-up sessions marked in green. The bootcamp for non-incoming international students ends Friday at noon.
  • The lessons are labeled with the initials of their primary author/presenter from the course staff.
  • There will be a special pre-bootcamp session on Saturday afternoon (Sept. 10) from 2-3:30 pm in room 115 of the Beckman Institute for students who are unable to complete Lesson 0.
